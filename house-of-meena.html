The Story of the Weave

Every saree begins on a wooden loom in Kanchipuram.

It takes a master weaver and an assistant up to three weeks to complete a single Kanjivaram — interlocking body and border in the rare korvai technique, where two colours meet without a seam.

The gold you see is not printed. It is real zari: fine silk thread wrapped in silver and dipped in gold, woven by hand, thread by thread.
