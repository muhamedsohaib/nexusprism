# Nexus Prism Homepage Wireframe + Copy Direction

Status: Wireframe and copy only. No implementation approved yet.
Branch: redesign-2026-luxury-command-center

## Approved inputs from founder

- Brand name for now: Nexus Prism.
- Final brand name: still undecided.
- Logo: approved uploaded prism logo with pearl-marble crystal, chrome/glass edges, gold vein detail, and black cinematic background.
- Color direction: black + red + pearl white.
- Entry screen CTA: Check Your Actual Worth.
- Hero H1: The AI operating layer for UAE sellers.
- Pricing posture: public starter pricing accepted.
- Main target: Amazon.ae sellers first.

The website must feel like a premium consulting firm that uses advanced AI systems, not a cheap AI agency and not a finished SaaS product pretending to have more than it has. The public website should sell the free audit first, then convert the right sellers into paid fix sprints and retainers.

# 3. AI Commerce Score Scanner

## Purpose

Make the free audit feel valuable, premium, and serious. This must not look like a normal contact form.

## Visual layout

Desktop:

- Left: explanation of what gets reviewed.
- Right: scanner form inside restrained glass card.
- Background: black with subtle red diagnostic lines.
- Form feels like an intake console.

Mobile:

- Explanation first.
- Scanner card below.
- Multi-step form if needed for usability.

## Scanner behavior

Step 1: Business identity.
Step 2: Store/listing details.
Step 3: Operational details.
Step 4: Main problem and consent.
Step 5: Submission confirmation + WhatsApp handoff.

## Required fields

- Business name.
- Contact name.
- WhatsApp number.
- Email.
- Store/listing URL.
- Main sales channel.
- Marketplace/store category.
- Top ASINs/SKUs/products if available.
- Monthly online revenue range.
- Main problem.
- Current tools/platforms used.
- Fulfillment method: FBA, FBN, FBM, own fulfillment, unknown.
- Brand registry/trademark status if relevant.
- Consent checkbox for manual review.

## Output promise

No instant fake score. The confirmation message should say the score will be manually reviewed and sent back.

# 9. FAQ

## Purpose

Remove fear and make the free score feel credible.

## Questions

1. Is the first Commerce Score really free?

Yes. The first review is free so we can see whether there is a real fix worth discussing.

2. Will I get an instant AI-generated score?

No. The first version is manually reviewed with AI support. That keeps the advice practical and avoids fake automated promises.

3. Do you only work with Amazon.ae sellers?

Amazon.ae sellers are the first focus, but the system is being built for noon, Shopify, and wider e-commerce operations as well.

4. Do I need to share private seller account access?

No for the first review. We start with visible links and information you choose to submit. Deeper access is only discussed if a paid sprint requires it.

5. Can you guarantee sales improvement?

No. We avoid fake guarantees. The goal is to identify and fix visible operational gaps that can improve the conditions for better performance.

6. Do you support Arabic?

Yes. The website and service direction are English and Arabic ready.

7. What happens after I submit my store?

Your request is stored, a WhatsApp handoff is opened, and your store is reviewed before a score and recommended next step are sent back.
