## Logo interpretation

The uploaded logo should drive the website's premium visual language:

- Pearl-marble prism material.
- Chrome / glass exterior edges.
- Gold vein detail.
- Black cinematic backdrop.
- Luxury object feel.
- Futuristic but not cyberpunk.

## Design implications

- Use the logo as the entry-screen centerpiece.
- Use prism-orb language in hero and dashboard preview.
- Keep red as a precision/scanner accent, not the main background.
- Keep pearl white for high-value typography and premium surfaces.
- Avoid generic purple AI gradients.
- Avoid fake client claims and fake performance promises.
